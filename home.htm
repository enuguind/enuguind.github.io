“ The blueprint for the industrialization of Nigeria.”

A center of both commerce and industry

The Enugu Industrial Park is designed to be one of the safest business destinations, a leading center for manufacturing, processing, and distribution of goods and services as well as world-class residential estates to live in Nigeria

. zones in the park .

World class!

Agricultural

The agricultural zone is a large area covering over 700 hectares of land for modern and high yield crop and livestock production with a produce market within the central area of the zone.

Beautiful Accomodation!

Residential

The residential zones will accommodate well serviced residential Estates with over 20,000 housing units comprising a few thousand well designed, finished and serviced Duplex mansions and several thousands of luxury flats most of which will be in high-rise apartment towers ranging from 10 to 35 floors high.

Great Shops!

Commercial

Modern commercial zones covering a modern 500 shop wholesale International Market, Shopping Malls, Long distance Transport Terminal, Sports and Recreational facilities, several hundreds of thousands of square meters of commercial office spaces on several high rise towers including a 60 floor tower which is proposed to be the tallest building in Africa, housing offices, hotels and several other business organizations.

Center of manufacturing!

industrial

The Enugu Industrial Park plans to host a Free Trade Zone in a delineated area within the Industrial zones and this shall confer special benefits and concessions to the free trade zone operators who are bringing in considerable foreign direct investment (FDI)
